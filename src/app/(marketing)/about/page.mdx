## Who we are

Sell Your House Free is a home-selling service for Arizona homeowners. We pair
every seller with a named Project Manager on our Arizona team and run the entire
transaction under JK Realty as broker of record.

We chose to start narrow on purpose. Seven AZ cities, Phoenix, Tucson, Mesa,
Chandler, Scottsdale, Gilbert, Glendale, is enough to build the trust, the PM
relationships, and the buyer pool that the service depends on. National scale
without that foundation is what every other “free” home-selling pitch turns out
to mean.

## Broker of record

Every listing and every transaction on this site is brokered by JK Realty under
the {SITE.broker.stateOfRecord} licensure of record. The brokerage name and license
number appear on the listing agreements and at the footer of every page on this
site.

## What we do

- We list homes on the Arizona MLS under JK Realty for zero seller commission.
- We bring a vetted, limited investor-buyer pool for sellers who want a fast cash close.
- We coordinate Cash+ through partner investors who fund pre-list improvements.
- We run our Renovation path with Hola Home Services as the renovation partner.
- We staff every sale with a real, named, reachable Project Manager.

## What we keep off this site

- We keep your contact information inside the engagement with your PM. We do not sell, broker, or license it to outside agents, investors, or marketers.
- We run only first-party Vercel analytics in production. No third-party tracking pixels, no session replay, no retargeting.
- We do not take referral revenue from outside agents or services we recommend.
- We do not run a chat widget or a newsletter signup; the only conversion path is `/get-offer`.
