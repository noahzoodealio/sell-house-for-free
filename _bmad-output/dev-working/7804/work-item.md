Hints keep both sides flexible.</li><li><strong>Why NOT a single <code><Pillar></code> generic component.</strong> Tempting: <code><Pillar slug="listing" hero=… steps=… body=… /></code>. Don't force one pattern per pillar; choose per page.</li><li><strong>Don't invent buyer-broker commission numbers.</strong> The copy may reference buyer-broker commission as "standard market practice" without naming a percentage.
